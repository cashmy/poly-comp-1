# Polymorphic Component

An exercise in coding out a polymorphic "text" component.

It includes "utility" functions that can be reused for repetitive component design.

Example:

### Output . . . . . . . Code 

`<text />  . . . . . <Text />`

`<h1 /> . . . . . . <Text as="h1" />`

`<h2 /> . . . . . . <Text as="h2" />`

`<div /> . . . . . . <Text as="div" />`
